	- Transition matrix from S to T

5. Orthogonality
	- Orthogonal bases
	- Orthonormal bases
	- Orthogonal matrices
6. Diagonalization
	- Eigenvalues and eigenvectors
	- Diagonalization
	- Orthogonal diagonalization
7. Linear transformation
